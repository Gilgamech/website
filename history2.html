  • Powers: Groups of humans which perform historical events and/or have an impact on other humans, and meet the following criteria. May also be a Polity.
    • Limited but functional government (maybe just one person)
    • Well-defined boundaries, that may be highly mobile (i.e. geographical borders of the currently-inhabited area).
    • Shared defense and resources.
    • Common goals and mutual interest.
    Examples:
    • Empire: Power large enough that one person alone cannot effectively provide goverance, and governmental power is split between several individuals.
      • Horizontal Empire: Split your state in half and give an ally full control over that half, but you're still friends.
      • Vertical Empire: Get an ally to handle the day-to-day tasks while you focus on larger strategic matters.
      • Federation: Vertical empire with multiple allies split horizontally as peers.
      • Confederacy: Horizontal empire with a friendship club of leaders.
    • Micropower: Small or medium group of mobile humans, like a mobile nation or state.
      • Government is often one person or a small committee.
      • Boundaries may be highly mobile.
